About the Studio

Built by people who'd rather touch a material than describe it.

Halden Studio started in 2021 as a two-person practice working out of a converted workshop in Kigali. We still work the same way: fewer projects, more time in each room, and a habit of choosing finishes in person rather than off a swatch card.

How we work

Every project starts with a Room Read — a walkthrough where we talk about how a space is actually used before anything is proposed. From there we build a material palette specific to that room's light, then the layout follows.

We keep a small studio on purpose. Founder Amara Halden leads design on every project alongside a rotating team of three to five collaborators, so nothing gets handed off without someone who was in the room from day one.

Where we've worked

Residential projects across Kigali, Nairobi, and Kampala, plus a handful of boutique hospitality spaces — cafés, a six-room guesthouse, and one dental practice that didn't want to feel like one.
